Description

A quick and light pasta dish that combines spaghetti, garlic, cherry tomatoes, olive oil and a touch of lemon — perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 175 g spaghetti
  • 175 g cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • 50 ml olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, sliced or minced
  • Zest and a few drops of juice from 1 lemon
  • Parsley, chopped (to taste)
  • Salt (for pasta and to taste)

How to make it

  1. Boil the spaghetti in plenty of salted water until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a skillet and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add halved tomatoes and lemon zest, cook gently ~6–8 minutes until they soften.
  4. Toss the drained spaghetti into the skillet, mix well and finish with lemon juice and chopped parsley.
  5. Serve immediately.
