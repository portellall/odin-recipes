Lasagna Recipe

Homepage Grilled Chicken Spaghetti

Description

Lasagna is one of the most classic and comforting dishes in Italian cuisine, known for its generous layers of pasta stacked with flavorful sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of fillings. Traditionally made with Bolognese sauce and plenty of mozzarella, it blends textures and flavors in a harmonious way, resulting in a rich and satisfying meal. Its preparation takes time and care, but the final result is worth it: a creamy, golden, aromatic dish that pleases everyone and becomes the highlight of any table, whether for family lunches or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic gloves, minced
  • 1 can of peeled tomatoes
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • ½ cup chopped parsley or cilantro
  • 1 package of pre-cooked lasagna noodles
  • 300 g sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 200 g sliced ham
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups milk

How to make it

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan
  2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden
  3. Add the garlic and ground beef, and cook until the meat is fully browned
  4. Once the meat is cooked, add the canned tomatoes and sauté a little longer
  5. In a baking dish, start layering: a layer of sauce, then lasagna noodles, then ham, mozzarella, and more sauce
  6. Repeat the layers until you run out of ingredients
  7. Finish with a layer of sauce and plenty of grated Parmesan cheese on top
  8. Bake in a medium oven (around 350F or 180C) until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese on top is melted and lightly golden
  9. Lei it rest for a few minutes before slicing - this helps the lasagna hold its shape
